Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

HYDEOUT is currently in internal testing. This Privacy Policy explains how HYDEOUT collects, uses, stores, and protects information while the app is being developed and tested.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use HYDEOUT:

  • Account information such as your email address, name, and authentication details.
  • Profile information such as your selected role, music preferences, event interests, city, region, or location preferences.
  • Location-related information, only when you allow location access or select a location manually.
  • App usage information such as saved events, followed artists, followed collectives, and interactions inside the app.
  • Device and technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, and diagnostic data.
  • Push notification tokens, if notifications are enabled.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Show relevant events, artists, collectives, venues, and nightlife-related content.
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • Test features during internal testing.
  • Send important app-related notifications, if enabled.
  • Detect, prevent, and fix technical issues.

3. Location Data

HYDEOUT may use your selected city, region, or device location to help show events and content near you. If device location access is requested, you can allow or deny it through your device settings. You may also choose or change your preferred location manually inside the app where supported.

4. Third-Party Services

HYDEOUT may use trusted third-party services to support authentication, hosting, database storage, analytics, crash reporting, notifications, and app testing. These services may process information on our behalf only as needed to provide and improve the app.

These services may include:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend services.
  • Apple Sign-In, if used for login.
  • Google Sign-In, if used for login.
  • Firebase or similar services, if used for analytics, crash reporting, or notifications.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate, test, secure, and improve HYDEOUT. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or security of users, the app, or others.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no digital service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep information only for as long as needed to provide, test, improve, or secure HYDEOUT, unless a longer retention period is required by law. You may request deletion of your account or personal information by contacting us.

8. User Choices

You may update certain account or profile information inside the app where supported. You can control permissions such as location and notifications through your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

HYDEOUT is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

10. Internal Testing Notice

HYDEOUT is currently being tested and may contain unfinished features, limited functionality, or test data. Information collected during testing is used to improve the app before public release.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
