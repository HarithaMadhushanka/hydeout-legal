Delete HYDEOUT Account

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

This page explains how HYDEOUT users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your HYDEOUT account, send an email to:

Email: hydeoutapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address used for your HYDEOUT account so we can identify and process the request.

What Will Be Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we will delete or anonymize account data associated with your HYDEOUT account, including:

  • Account information such as your email address and profile details.
  • Selected role, city, region, location preferences, and music preferences.
  • Saved events, followed artists, followed collectives, and other app activity linked to your account.
  • Push notification tokens linked to your account, if applicable.

Data We May Retain

Some limited information may be retained when necessary for security, fraud prevention, debugging, legal compliance, or record keeping. Any retained information will only be kept for as long as reasonably necessary.

Processing Time

We aim to process account deletion requests within a reasonable time after verifying the request.

Alternative In-App Method

If available, you may also request account deletion from inside the HYDEOUT app through the account or settings section.
